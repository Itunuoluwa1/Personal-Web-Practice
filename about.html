Why die silently when you can always get consultations?

It has come to my awareness that people die due to inability to get consultations because of certain reasons:

  • The nature of some people's job gives them little chance to attend to what really matters, their health. It's usually cumbersome for them to attend to work and still monitor their health.
  • Some people are unable to get medical attention due to distance and the location that they might be.
  • Also, some people are due to age. It's not easy for an aged person to be going to an hospital on regular basis whereas, they need medical attendance.
  • However, some don't get medical attendance because of ignorance and even procrastination. This is the most unexplainable reason but still the commonest occurence.

Digital Diagnosis Site was oriented to create interactions between health practitioners and people in need of consultations. On DDS, you can easily get a doctor's appointment and meet with the doctor all online without batting an eyelid. One can as well get similar illness prescription for trivial matters. They'd be regular medical assistance for old people.

Our Mission:
To ensure people are getting proper medical attention and to make consultants accesible.

Our Vision:
To resolve health problems and to reduce the national death rate.
